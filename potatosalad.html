World's best potato salad

Image of tasty potato salad

Description

The best ever potato salad is cremier, richest potato salad you will ever try.

Ingedients

  • 6 medium potato
  • half cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon of constarch

Directions

Place potatos ina large pot and cover with salted water; bring it to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain and set aside to cool.
